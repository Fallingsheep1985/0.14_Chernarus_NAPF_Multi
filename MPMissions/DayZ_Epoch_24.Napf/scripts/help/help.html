Help and Information


You need a Radio to use DZGM group chat this will also protect you from EVR Blowout.

Right click toolbox to deploy a bike that can be upgraded.

Right click a Bloodbag to Bloodbag yourself.

Right click a secondary weapon to commit suicide.

Right click a Knife near a hemp field to harvest it.

Right click a Emerald to build furniture for your base

You can buy gems from food traders.

Press F10 to toggle debugmonitor.

Press J to open your Journal.

You can add NOS to your car with a jerrycan and a can of Redbull.

You can load zombies into trucks and transport them then unload them.

Right click Weapons to add/remove attachments (attachments dont save between restarts).

You can arrest players and escort/serach them if you have scrapmetal.

Right click binoculars to change your view distance.
